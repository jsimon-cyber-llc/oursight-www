Ownership & privacy

Your film is yours. Your players' privacy is the point.

Your 8K master lives in your cloud, DRM-free, and exports anytime. No quota, no contract between you and your footage. And when a roster includes minors, restraint is a feature: per-purpose consent you can grant or revoke, export or delete on request, and access scoped to each family. Clarity is the safety mechanism.

From the founder

I built OurSight after a tournament weekend — watching how much my players had grown all season, and realizing I didn't truly understand game film myself until I was about 20.
