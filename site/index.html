The problem

Your film shouldn't be hostage to your hardware.

Most lacrosse programs rent their way into a corner. A $1,300–$1,900 camera that needs an active subscription to do its job. Footage that lands the next day, after the teaching moment is gone. Drawings you can't pull out of the platform. Pricing you can only learn by getting on a call. And film that lives behind someone else's storage quota and contract — so the day you leave, you're negotiating to get your own season back.

None of that makes your players better. It just makes leaving expensive.

OurSight starts from the opposite assumption: the film is yours, the camera is a commodity you own, and the price is on the page.

How it works

The hardware

A $400 camera you own — not a $1,300 one you rent.

The whole-field, both-ways, zoom-and-draw view isn't a hardware problem — it's a resolution problem we solve in software. A fixed wide 8K master plus a virtual-camera crop means the camera stays a cheap commodity you buy once and own outright. No lease, no return-on-cancel, no "subscription required" to make the hardware work. The camera is an accessory. The software and the lacrosse data model are the product.

  • Camera — Insta360 Ace Pro 2, native 8K, flat 157° lens ~$400
  • Storage — 512GB card included
  • Power — USB-C power bank for full-game recording included
  • Mount — 15–20 ft midfield pole included
  • All-in — bought once, owned forever ≈ $520

Ownership & privacy

Your film is yours. Your players' privacy is the point.

The original 8K master lives in your cloud, DRM-free, and exports anytime — there's no quota deciding which seasons you're allowed to keep, and no contract standing between you and your own footage. And because minors use this product, restraint is a feature: a visible COPPA/FERPA consent ledger, export and delete on request, and redact-on-export keep parents informed and in control. Clarity is the safety mechanism.

  • Export your master and your telestration anytime — no claw-back
  • Visible COPPA/FERPA consent ledger; export or delete on request
  • Redact-on-export for anything that leaves the platform
  • Multi-tenant security with enforced row-level isolation per org
